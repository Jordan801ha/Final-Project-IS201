MOTOCROSS: MY RACING JOURNEY

History of Motocross

Motocross began in the early 1900s in the United Kingdom as off-road motorcycle races known as "scrambles." Over the decades, it evolved into a competitive sport with dirt tracks, high jumps, and challenging terrain. I’ve personally been involved in motocross, desert racing, and grand prix races for years.
